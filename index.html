About Me

Hi, my name is Aaron Puchalski, I am 22 years old and am currently attending the University of Hawaii at West Oahu, majoring in General Creative Media. You’ll usually find me watching movies (I am a huge movie nerd, so much so that I have a huge Blu-Ray collection), catching up on my TV shows, or hitting the gym. I love to edit video and I find a lot of passion behind writing movie reviews, putting my thoughts out of what I’ve watched recently. I aspire to be one of the great directors, writers, producers, my biggest inspirations being Denis Villeneuve, Ryan Coogler, The Daniels, and Jon M. Chu. If anything, I would love to find my career working in the movies, whether my part be big or small.
